# Product Brief

**LexNova Legal** is a PWA-ready platform for a Lithuania-focused law firm, specializing in immigration, business setup, and employment law.

## Target Audience
- Foreign investors and entrepreneurs looking to start businesses in Lithuania.
- Non-EU citizens seeking immigration services (visas, TRP).
- Local companies needing employment law compliance.

## Primary Conversions
1. **Consultation Leads**: Booking a legal consultation via form.
2. **Direct Contact**: Click-to-call or WhatsApp engagement.

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)
- **Public Site**: High-performance landing page + services pages.
- **Blog**: SEO-optimized legal articles.
- **Admin CMS**: Simple dashboard for managing blog posts and viewing leads.
- **Lead Intake**: Contact forms with email notifications.
