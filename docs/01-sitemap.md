# Sitemap

## Public Routes
- `/` (Home): Value prop, trust signals, services overview.
- `/services`: List of practice areas.
- `/services/[slug]`: Detailed service page (e.g., Business Setup).
- `/blog`: Latest legal updates and articles.
- `/blog/[slug]`: Article detail page.
- `/about`: Firm history and lawyer profiles.
- `/contact`: Lead intake form and contact details.

## Admin Routes
- `/admin/login`: Secure entry.
- `/admin/dashboard`: Overview of activity.
- `/admin/blog`: CRUD for blog posts.
- `/admin/leads`: View intake form submissions.
- `/admin/settings`: Site configuration.

## Navigation Rules
- **Header**: Logo (Home), Services, Blog, About, Contact (CTA Button).
- **Footer**: Quick links, Socials, Legal Disclaimers, Contact info.
