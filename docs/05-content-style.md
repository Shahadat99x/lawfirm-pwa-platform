# Content Style Guide

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional but Accessible**: Avoid dense legalese where possible.
- **Empathetic**: Understand the stress of immigration/legal issues.
- **Clear & Direct**: Get to the point.

## Microcopy
- **Errors**: Helpful and specific (e.g., "Please enter a valid email" instead of "Error").
- **Buttons**: Action-oriented (e.g., "Book Consultation", "Get Started").

## Disclaimers
> [!IMPORTANT]
> All informational pages must identify as "General Information", not "Legal Advice".
- Footer must contain standard disclaimer.
